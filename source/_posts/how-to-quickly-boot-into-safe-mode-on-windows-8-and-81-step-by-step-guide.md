## How to Quickly Boot Into Safe Mode on Windows 8 and 8.1 - Step-by-Step Guide!

Sometimes you may need to enter Safe Mode to troubleshoot some software or drivers that cause serious PC problems. If your PC is running Windows 8 or Windows 8.1, you can refer steps here to enter Safe Mode.  
  
 If Windows won’t boot properly, please follow these steps to enter Safe Mode.  
  
 1\. Power up your PC more than 2 times until you see Windows screen display “Preparing Automatic Repair” then “Diagnosing” your PC”. After this, the Startup Repair window will appear.

 ![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/img_56ebc90860567.png) 

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/img_56ebc912c08c2.png) 

  
2\. Click **Advanced options**  , then the system will direct the display to Windows RE (Recovery environment.)  
  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/img_56ebc9196174e.png) 

  
 3\. In Windows RE (Recovery environment), click **Troubleshoot** .   
  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/img_56ebc921a51fb.png)   
  
 4\. Click **Advanced options** .  
  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/img_56ebc92d07e37.png) 

  
5\. Click **Startup Settings**  .  
  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/img_56ebc9371f152.png)   
  
 6\. Click **Restart**  button.  
  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/img_56ebc93b541ff.png) 

  
7\. You can press **4**  number key to enter Safe Mode. (If you need Internet access, press **5**  number key to enter Safe Mode with Networking.)  
  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/img_56ebc941f1993.png) 

  
 If you can enter Windows Normal Mode, please follow steps below.

1\. Press the **Windows** key ![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/img_56ebca84337f7.png)  \+ **C** , and then click **Settings** .   
  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/img_56ebca93e2b70.png) 

  
2\. Click **Power** , hold down **Shift** on your keyboard and then click **Restart** .   
  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/img_56ebca98ac830.png)   
  
 3\. In Windows RE (Recovery environment), click **Troubleshoot** .  
  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/img_56ebca9dea91e.png) 

  
 4\. Click **Advanced options**  .  
  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/img_56ebcaa466824.png) 

  
5\. Click **Startup Settings** .   
  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/img_56ebcaab88c7a.png) 

  
 6\. Click **Restart** button.   
  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/img_56ebcab097ade.png)   
  
 7\. You can press **4**  number key to enter Safe Mode. (If you need Internet access, press**5** to enter Safe Mode with Networking.)  
  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/03/img_56ebcab571726.png)
