## **Option 2: Disable IPv6 in Network Center**

 1) On your keyboard, press**Windows key** and**X** at the same time, then choose**Network Connections** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592f99d858612.png)

 2) Choose**Network and Sharing Center** on the right side of the pane.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592fb0061fd96.png)

 3) Find your connection, then click it to view its detailed information.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592fb05393ff0.png)

 4) Click**Properties** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592fb06cd3856.png)

 5) Locate**Internet Protocol Version 6 (TCP /IPv6)** . Click to**un-tick** the box before this option. Then click**OK** to save the change.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592fb092b47aa.png)

6) Restart your PC to see if this problem continues.

## **Option 3: Disable Network List Service**

 1) On your keyboard, hit**Windows key** and**R** at the same time. Then type in**services.msc** and hit**Enter** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592f9130cba51.png)

 2) Press**N** key on your keyboard to faster locate the **Network List Service** service from the list. Right click it and choose**Properties** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592f97e676a96.jpg)

 3) In the**Startup type** category, choose from the drop down menu**Disable** .

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/img_592f9852c81ec.png)
