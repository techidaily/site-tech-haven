iMessage appeared to be restored after blacking out globally. [According to Downdetector.com,](https://downdetector.com/status/imessage/) thousands of Apple device users reported experiencing issues while using iMessage on Thursday afternoon.

Many reported they saw the "not delivered" flag while trying to send texts using the chat service. The tracking site received over 13,000 reports of iMessage not working properly.
